Our Story

A Lifetime of Coffee,
A Legacy of Care

For over fifty years, Hendro Greeff has started every morning the same way — with a great cup of coffee. Through decades of travel across continents, he's tasted beans from the mountains of Colombia to the highlands of Ethiopia, developing a palate shaped by curiosity, experience, and an uncompromising love for the craft.

The name Herr Baron is a tribute to the Greeff family's German heritage — an homage to old-world character, tradition, and the quiet dignity of doing things properly. It's not about titles. It's about a standard.

Together with his beloved wife Ina, Hendro still runs Abendruhe Old Age Home in Pretoria, pouring the same care and warmth into their community as they do into every bag of Herr Baron Coffee. Great coffee, like great company, is meant to be shared.

— Hendro & Ina Greeff
